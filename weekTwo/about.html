About Me

Hi there, my name is Eric and I'm currently a full-time student at Pace University's NYC campus. I'm in my fourth year of college and I'm an IT major. Additionally, I love to do crafty things - interior design/decorating, drawing and painting, designing websites -- you name it and I'm probably interested.
